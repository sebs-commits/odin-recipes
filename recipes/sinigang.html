How to make sinigang

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 1 bunch spinach
  • 3 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 12 pieces string beans
  • 2 pieces tomato
  • 3 pieces chili or banana pepper
  • 1 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 quarts water
  • 1 piece sliced onion
  • 2 pieces taro gabi
  • 1 pack sinigang mix
